Galaxy Warriors

An Homage to 16-bit Arcade style Space shooters with plenty of features

Made as a Collaboration project for BUR\WEB by the Extremely Talented:
  • Kasmi Mohamed Iyad – Main Project Developer and Designer
  • Zaidi Amine Abderahmane – Assets Provider
  • Djemma Aymen – Play-Testing and Bug-Hunting

Feature List

  • Intuitive Controls: Smooth movement with the Arrow keys and Shoot with SPACE
  • Functional and Aesthetic Interface: Tutorial and Settings sections
  • Score System: Each enemy killed gives you 10 points
  • Level System: Levels increase based on your score
  • Enemy Variety: 6 types of enemies:
    • Greens: Move straight down
    • Blues: Move in a zigzag
    • Reds: Stay still and shoot at you
    • Type 1: one hit to kill
    • Type 2: two hits to kill
  • Level Progression:
    • Level 1: only greens
    • Level 2: only blues
    • Level 3: only reds
    • Level 4: only type 1
    • Level 5+: all types mixed
